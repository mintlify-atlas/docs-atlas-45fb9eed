The Lesson Library provides five curated conversation modules that give the AI a specific role and goal. Each lesson targets real-world vocabulary and cultural context, from ordering coffee to conducting a business meeting.

Open the library by clicking **Library** in the top-right header.

## Free Talk Mode

The default mode. No structured scenario is applied — the AI acts as a general Mandarin conversation partner and adjusts to any topic you raise. Best for exploring vocabulary and building fluency without constraints.

## Lessons

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Self Introduction — Beginner · Social">
    **Goal:** Practice introducing yourself — name, nationality, and profession.

    The AI plays a new acquaintance at a social gathering. It will:
    1. Ask your name (你叫什么名字？)
    2. Ask where you're from (你是哪国人？)
    3. Ask what you do for work (你做什么工作？)

    Gentle grammar corrections are provided throughout. Sentences are kept short and simple.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ordering Coffee — Beginner · Daily Life">
    **Goal:** Practice ordering drinks, specifying size, sugar and ice levels, and paying.

    The AI plays a barista. It will:
    1. Welcome you and ask what you'd like (你好，要喝点什么？)
    2. Ask about temperature (hot/iced) and size
    3. Ask about sugar preference
    4. State the price and request payment (WeChat Pay or Alipay)

    Key vocabulary: 咖啡 (coffee), 冰 (ice), 热 (hot), 杯 (cup), 扫码 (scan code).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Taking a Taxi — Intermediate · Travel">
    **Goal:** Give directions to a driver, discuss routes, and handle payment.

    The AI plays a Beijing taxi driver. It will:
    1. Ask where you're going (师傅，去哪儿？)
    2. Ask for a nearby landmark if it doesn't know the exact street
    3. Chat about traffic (堵车) or weather during the ride
    4. Ask for the fare on arrival

    Encourages use of direction words: 左转 (turn left), 右转 (turn right), 直走 (go straight).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bargaining at the Market — Intermediate · Shopping">
    **Goal:** Buy fruit or souvenirs and negotiate a lower price.

    The AI plays an enthusiastic street vendor. It will:
    1. Offer goods enthusiastically (来看一看，很便宜！)
    2. Quote a slightly high price
    3. React with mock shock to your counter-offer
    4. Eventually agree to a middle price

    Tone is fun and lively.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Business Meeting — Advanced · Professional">
    **Goal:** Practice formal greetings, business card exchange, and schedule discussion.

    The AI plays a manager at a Chinese tech company. It will:
    1. Use formal language (您, 幸会)
    2. Simulate exchanging business cards
    3. Discuss the agenda for the afternoon meeting
    4. Ask about accommodation arrangements

    Focus on formal business etiquette and vocabulary.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Difficulty levels

| Level | Color | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Beginner | Green | Simple vocabulary, short sentences, frequent corrections |
| Intermediate | Yellow | Real-world scenarios, directional and transactional language |
| Advanced | Red | Formal register, professional vocabulary, cultural nuance |

## Selecting a lesson

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Library">
    Click the **Library** button in the top-right header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse lessons">
    Review the lesson cards. Each shows the title, level badge, topic, and a short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a lesson">
    Click any lesson card to activate it. The active lesson is highlighted and its name appears in the header as a pill badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the session">
    Press **Start Conversation**. The AI will receive the lesson scenario as its system context and begin the roleplay immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Switching lessons while a session is active will disconnect you first. Make sure you're done with your current conversation before switching scenarios.
</Warning>

## The Lesson interface

```typescript types.ts
export interface Lesson {
  id: string;          // Unique identifier (e.g. 'coffee', 'taxi')
  title: string;       // Display name
  description: string; // Short summary shown in the lesson card
  level: 'Beginner' | 'Intermediate' | 'Advanced';
  topic: string;       // Category (e.g. 'Travel', 'Shopping')
  scenario: string;    // The full roleplay instruction sent to the AI
}
```

The `scenario` field is passed as part of the system instruction when `LiveApiService.connect(scenarioInstruction)` is called.
